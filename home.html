Frequently Asked Questions

How is Jobsess different from competitors?

While traditional job boards rely on keyword search, our industry-leading AI matching technology finds candidates with the right skills and experience for your job and actively invites them to apply.

How does Jobsess distribute my job posting?

Jobs are distributed to 100+ top job boards and millions of candidates through daily job alert emails and our mobile apps in order to find you the most qualified candidates as quickly as possible.

How long does it take?

Currently, 80% of employers who post a job on Jobsess get a quality candidate through the site within the first day. Until you close your job ad, we will continue to match your job with potential candidates and notify them to apply.

Is there a limit on candidates I can receive?

No. We never limit the number of candidates or applications you receive.

What is the commitment?

Your commitment depends on which hiring plan you choose. To try Standard or Premium for free, click here. Interested in a Pro plan? Live chat, email us or call (877) 252-1062. Click here for enterprise solutions.

Can I increase/decrease my number of job postings at any time?

Yes. Simply head over to your Account page. From there, you can adjust your plan as needed.
